Privacy & Security

Your trust is our most important asset. Here's how we protect your data.

Our Philosophy

QuMail is designed from the ground up with a single mission: to provide a secure layer of end-to-end encryption over your existing email accounts. We believe you shouldn't have to choose between convenience and privacy. We never handle your passwords, and we can't read your encrypted messages.

Secure Authentication via OAuth 2.0

When you sign in with Google, Microsoft, or Yahoo, you are using the industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocol. This means you authenticate directly with your email provider. QuMail receives a secure token to access your account but never sees or stores your password. This ensures your primary account credentials remain completely private.

End-to-End Encryption with AES-GCM

The core of QuMail is its on-demand encryption. When you choose to send an encrypted email, the message body and any attachments are encrypted on your device before they are sent. We use AES-GCM, a modern, authenticated encryption cipher, to ensure both confidentiality and integrity. Only the recipient with the correct decryption key can view the original content.

Ephemeral Key Management

To manage decryption, QuMail uses a local SQLite database on your device to store the necessary ephemeral encryption keys. These keys are temporary and are only used for decrypting the messages intended for you. This decentralized approach means we do not have a central repository of keys, making your encrypted communication significantly more secure.

What We Don't Collect

We are committed to data minimization. We do not track your activity, sell your data, or analyze your email content for advertising. Our business is protecting your privacy, not profiting from your data.
